Issue Reporting and Tracking - JIRA

Please use JIRA for reporting any issue and tracking their progress. In addition, Jira offers many additonal (more sophisticated) features that allow you to keep in touch with the project's current progress.

Before submitting issues, please read 'How to submit an issue'.

Subversion access

The Codehaus hosts five active top level projects, including Castor itself (main) and a couple of sub-projects within Castor main. The source code, documentation and libraries of the top level projects are available from Subversion.

Castor (main)

To checkout the Castor-main source as a committer: 

 $ svn co https://svn.codehaus.org/castor/castor/trunk

To checkout the Castor-main source anonymously:

 $ svn co http://svn.codehaus.org/castor/castor/trunk

To browse the Castor-main source:

 $ http://svn.codehaus.org/castor/castor/trunk

If you happen to be behind a (corporate) firewall and thus are not able to use SVN, one of the following snapshots can also be downloaded (using FishEye):

-ZIP archive
-TGZ archive
-TBZ2 archive

Alternatively, download the full source distribution for a given release.

Related projects

There is four more top-level projects related to Castor, that can be checked out from the following URLs:

-castor-jaxb-2.0 

  $ svn co http://svn.codehaus.org/castor/castor-jaxb-2.0/trunk

-castor-jpa 

  $ svn co http://svn.codehaus.org/castor/castor-jpa/trunk

-castor-spring 

  $ svn co http://svn.codehaus.org/castor/castor-spring/trunk

-spring-xml 

  $ svn co http://svn.codehaus.org/castor/spring-xml/trunk

Information on Subversion

For more information about Subversion and various tools to access Subversion repositories you may want to take a look at Subversion site.
